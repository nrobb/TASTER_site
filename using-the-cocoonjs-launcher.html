Accessing the game through the CocoonJS launcher.

The CocoonJS launcher is a free app that allows you to test HTML5 games on Android and iOS. To use the lanucher, follow these instructions:

  1. Download and install the launcher. It is available for Android and iOS devices.

  2. Start the launcher. Select YOUR APP.

  3. You will be asked to log in. An account has been created for you, and your login details will have been sent to you by email.

  4. Tap "Select a URL to a zip or html file", and enter the following: nrobb.github.io/taster

  5. Select CANVAS+ to load and play the game.

  6. The CocoonJS Launcher displays debugging information in the top left corner by default. To hide this, click on the gear icon to access the settings, and set 'Enable Debug' to 'Off'.
