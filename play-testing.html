Play testing of our first prototype game

The first prototype of our game is now ready for play testing. This is a beta version of the software, so it may have some bugs. We'd like our participants to try the game for a short time, and tell us what they think of it.

The game can be accessed in several ways:
  • Android users can download the game for free from Google Play.
  • iOS users can access the game through the CocoonJS launcher app. Full instructions on how to do this can be found here.
  • The game can also be played in most web browsers (either on a tablet or a computer) at this link.
